# which is what
this is self-reminder notes

## what is "/bake"?
**Bake** is a category of bakable items with the `smoker` or the `campfire`.
Subset: Food
Ex. Chorus Fruit counted as Food

## what is "/melt"?
**Melt** is a category of meltable items with the `blast furnace`.
Subset: Ore = {Mineral Ore, Raw Metal}, Metal Gear
Ex. Glass counted as Mineral

## what is "/general"?
`General` is a category of generic smeltable items with only `furnace`.
Also known as Furnace-Only
Ex. Charcoal counted as Non-Mineral Coal
